Integritetspolicy

Detta dokument beskriver informationen som samlas in av Nightscout Status,
https://nielsmaerten.github.io/nightscout-assistant ("Tjänsten" eller "Mjukvaran") och hur denna information används.

Vilken information samlas in?

The Service needs 2 things in order to work: the email address of your Google Account (so it can know it's you when you make a request),
and the URL of your Nightscout instance (so it can look up your glucose and read it to you).

How is this information used?

When you invoke the Service through Google Assistant (i.e.: by saying: 'Hey Google, talk to Nightscout Status),
the Service looks at your email address, and checks if there's a Nightscout site associated with it.
If so, your current glucose level is read from your Nightscout site.

Sharing of information

Your email address or Nightscout URL will never be shared with a third party.
I just need it so I know which Nightscout site to check when you invoke the Action.
Data from your Nightscout website is not saved, not even to log files.

Deleting your data

You can choose to revoke access at any time: Simply via log in, remove your Nightscout URL, and click Save.
You can also remove Nightscout Status from your Google Account.

Analytics Cookie

After you sign in to this site, some anonymous statistics about your visit are collected using a cookie (Google Analytics).
The cookie is not enabled until after you've accepted this Privacy Policy.
I'm using Analytics to better understand how people are using this site, how they found it, and which demographic they're in.
This only applies to https://nielsmaerten.github.io, not to usage of the Assistant Action itself.
If you'd like to opt-out of the Google Analytics cookie, you can do so by visiting https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout

Healthcare information disclaimer

The information provided by Nightscout Status is not a substitute for advice from a medical professional.
